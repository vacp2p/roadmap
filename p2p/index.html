P2P Service Unit

P2P Service Unit

The P2P Service unit is responsible for developing nim-libp2p, which is utilized across various IFT projects. Additionally, our team conducts research to enhance libp2p protocols, with a particular emphasis on improving gossipsub. Major future work items on nim-libp2p (also beyond this reporting period are captured on github.
