## Description

This commitment involves demonstrating completed work and creating presentations to showcase the Web team's deliverables, share progress with the entire organization, and gather feedback for continuous improvement. These demonstrations serve as internal communication tools that present what the team has accomplished and enable cross-functional collaboration and learning.

The demos commitment supports IFT's strategic objectives by:
- **Completed Work Showcase**: Demonstrating finished Web team deliverables and accomplishments to the broader organization
- **Progress Presentations**: Sharing completed projects, features, and solutions through organized presentations
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Communicating technical approaches, solutions, and learnings from completed work across service units and projects
- **Feedback Collection**: Gathering input from stakeholders, other teams, and the organization on completed work to improve future deliverables and alignment
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Enabling other teams to understand Web capabilities and identify collaboration opportunities based on demonstrated work
