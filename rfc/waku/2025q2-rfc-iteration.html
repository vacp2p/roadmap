vac:rfc:waku:2025q2-rfc-iteration

Iteration commitments generally comprise: through discussion and curation, support the process of auditing raw, draft, mature selected Waku RFCs.

Description §

This commitment supports the audit of mature RFCs, which are either in draft or raw states. The audit comprises of:

  • Reading through the spec;
  • Checking the relevant codebase;
  • Changing the parts of the spec (descriptions and codebase) where applicable;
  • Updates of relevant links;
  • Changing the RFC status (optional);
  • Going through rounds of feedback with the relevant project team;
  • Adding clarifications where needed in the spec;
  • Checking for inconsistencies with other documentation/codebase.

The following RFCs will be worked on in this quarter:

  • 30/ADAPTIVE-NODES
  • 66/WAKU2-METADATA
  • 53/WAKU2-X3DH
  • 54/WAKU2-X3DH-SESSIONS
  • WAKU2-ENR
  • WAKU2-DEVICE-PAIRING

We will also include specification validation - editorial work through previous and future RFCs to improve them (identify edge cases for example)

Alignment with VAC Narratives:

  • The VAC RFC team plans to become a repository for RFCs regarding all IFT projects.
  • By promoting the RFC culture, we hope to incentivize projects to submit their RFCs to the rfc-editors team.
  • We aim to have the broader web3 community use our RFCs in their projects, similar to IETF.
  • We aim to provide feedback on specifications from the point of view of a new developer.
